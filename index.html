THE PARKER FILES

The Parker Files is an online archive that dives into the life, legacy, and hidden stories of Peter Parker from The Amazing Spider-Man 1 and 2. From personal discoveries to untold moments and the struggles behind the mask, this site provides narratives and visual snapshots that explore what it really means to carry great power and the responsibility that comes with it. This collection offers a closer look at the universe created in the two TASM films.

17 Years Old

High-school Peter, where he discovers his powers and takes his first steps as a hero. He is often bullied at school, very shy, yet brilliant. This reflects the original young Peter Parker from the early comics.

19 Years Old

Post-high school Peter, after discovering his powers, carries greater responsibility and begins to balance adult life with superhero work. He struggles to manage everything while wanting to protect both the city and his loved ones.

28 Years Old

Fully mature Peter Parker / Spider-Man, now more experienced in his role as a superhero, balances adult life with his heroic duties. He has learned from his past mistakes and continues to correct them, remaining the friendly neighborhood Spider-Man.

THE AMAZING SPIDER-MAN

An intellectually gifted yet socially introverted teenager struggling to find his place in life ever since his parents died when he was a child. When Peter sneaks inside the Oscorp building where his father worked, a genetically enhanced spider bites him on the back of his neck, giving him inhuman abilities, similar to that of a spider's.
